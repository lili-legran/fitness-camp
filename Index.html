How to Run Like a Girl

Women really do run the world. For nearly a decade, more women runners have been crossing the finish line than men. In the United States, women make up 57 percent of finishers — that's about 10.7 million women racing. Globally, female race participation is up 25 percent, compared to 7 percent for men. So ladies, grab the sports bra, lace up your sneakers and let’s hit the road.
